TXMODE Subroutine

 
Usage:
TXMODE (m)
 
Description:
Sets the video text mode. Setting the video mode is only relevant when used in the DOS environment.
    0=Black and White 40 columns;
    1=Color 40 columns;
    2=Black and White 80 columns;
    3=Color 80 columns;
    7=Monochrome 80 columns;
    64=EGA/VGA 43/50 lines.
